# Leap Year Checker

## Problem Statement
Given a year **Y**, determine whether it is a **leap year** or not.  

**Rules for Leap Year:**
1. A year is a leap year if it is divisible by **400**.  
2. A year is a leap year if it is divisible by **4** but **not divisible by 100**.  
3. All other years are **not leap years**.

## Input
An integer **Y** (year).
